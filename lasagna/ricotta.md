Ricotta mixture
===============

Notes
-----
* Most ricotta comes in 15oz containers, make sure you only use 12oz.

Ingredients
-----------
* 12 oz ricotta
* 1 large egg
* 1 tbsp dried parsley flakes
* 1/2 tsp dried oregano
* 1/4 tsp pepper
* 1/3 cup grated parmesan

Prep
----
* Slightly beat egg
* Grate parmesan

Directions
----------
1. Combine all ingredients in a medium-sized mixing bowl.
